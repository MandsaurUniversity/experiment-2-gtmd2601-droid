## Page 2: About (about.html)

### About the Department
The Department of Computer Applications was established in 2005 as part of the Faculty of Science and Technology at Mandsaur University. Starting with just 30 students and 3 faculty members, the department has grown remarkably over two decades.

Today, the department offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and diploma programs in computer applications. With a focus on practical learning and industry readiness, the department has established itself as one of the premier institutions for computer education in Madhya Pradesh.

### Vision
To become a nationally recognized centre of excellence in computer applications education and research, producing innovative and ethical technology professionals.

### Mission
1. To provide quality education in computer science and applications through an updated curriculum
2. To foster innovation and research among students and faculty
3. To develop industry partnerships for real-world exposure and placement opportunities
4. To create an inclusive learning environment that encourages creativity and critical thinking
5. To contribute to society through technology-driven solutions

### History Timeline
- **2005:** Department established with BCA program (30 students)
- **2008:** PGDCA program launched
- **2010:** MCA program introduced
- **2012:** First batch achieves 100% placement
- **2015:** Department awarded "Best CS Department" by MP Technical University
- **2018:** New computer labs inaugurated with 120 systems
- **2020:** Online teaching infrastructure developed during pandemic
- **2022:** MoU with TCS iON, Infosys Springboard
- **2024:** Ranked in Top 50 BCA departments in MP
- **2025:** Certificate courses in Web Development and Data Science launched

### Accreditation
- NAAC Accredited (Grade B++)
- Approved by AICTE, New Delhi
- Affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya (RGPV), Bhopal

## Page 3: Courses (courses.html)

### BCA — Bachelor of Computer Applications
- **Duration:** 3 Years (6 Semesters)
- **Eligibility:** 10+2 with Mathematics
- **Annual Fee:** ₹35,000
- **Seats:** 120
- **Subjects Include:** Programming in C, Data Structures, Web Technology, DBMS, Operating Systems, Java, Python, Software Engineering, Computer Networks
- **Career Options:** Software Developer, Web Developer, System Analyst, Database Administrator

### MCA — Master of Computer Applications
- **Duration:** 2 Years (4 Semesters)
- **Eligibility:** BCA/B.Sc (CS) with 50% marks
- **Annual Fee:** ₹45,000
- **Seats:** 60
- **Subjects Include:** Advanced Java, Cloud Computing, Machine Learning, Big Data Analytics, Cyber Security, Software Project Management
- **Career Options:** Senior Developer, Project Manager, Data Scientist, IT Consultant
